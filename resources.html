加薪谈话脚本Raise Conversation Script
Print, fill in your numbers, practice out loud打印 · 填入您的数字 · 大声练习
步骤Step 中文说法In Chinese 英语说法In English
① 预约① Book it 「我想谈谈我的职业发展，30 分钟方便吗？」"I'd like to discuss my career growth — do you have 30 minutes?" "Could we set up 30 minutes to talk about my career growth?"
② 感谢② Thanks 「谢谢您过去一年的指导。」"Thank you for your guidance this past year." "Thank you for your guidance over the past year — I've learned a lot here."
③ 列三件成绩③ 3 wins 「过去一年我做了 ___、___ 和 ___ 」"This year I did ___, ___, and ___." "This past year, I [#1], [#2], and [#3]."
④ 明确请求④ Specific ask 「我想请您考虑把我的工资从 ___ 调到 ___ 。」"I'd like you to consider raising my pay from ___ to ___." "Would you consider an increase from $___/hour to $___/hour?"
⑤ 如果不行⑤ If "no" 「请教您，下次能加薪我应该做哪些事？」"What would I need to show next time to earn a raise?" "What would I need to demonstrate by next review to make that possible?"
⑥ 收尾⑥ Close 「谢谢您 — 我会写邮件总结今天讨论的内容。」"Thank you — I'll send a quick email summary of what we discussed." "Thanks — I'll send you a quick email summary so we're on the same page."
💼 谈完一定要发后续邮件 — 把数字和承诺写下来，避免「老板忘了」。 💼 Always send a follow-up email — write down the numbers and any commitments to prevent "the boss forgot."

基于这些成绩，可以加到 ___ 吗？Based on these results, could you raise it to ___ ? "Based on these results, could you consider raising it to $___?" 提具体数字Stating your number
